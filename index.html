Introducing Pluto, the Virtual Assistant.

Human-robot

As a human personal assistant, our virtual assistant will also carry out ever work you ask it to do. Just unlike your human assistant it won’t have give you excuses for not getting your work done on time. 

Virtual Assistant of 2021

A virtual assistant is a software developed to make humans interaction with computer much easier. By listen to the owner’s command and running the desired command to save the user’s time for search, understand, and make decision faster. Getting your desired output on your fingertips will make your work accomplish faster and in an easier way.

Objectives
The objective of a virtual assistant is to listen to users’ voice command and speak out the desired and accepted reply. Letting the virtual assistant do your job without touching your keyboard and mouse.

Scope
Unlike other virtual assistants in market our assistant won’t share the user data to a cloud storage and make it less secure. Instead it will carry out every process by its own, without getting out to the cloud server to execute its operation.

Speech

User have to just speak out, for giving commands and in return it will give your solution in an audio format.

Quick access

Every feasible work of the computer will be given with simple speech command.

Display

If the user wants to communicate with the assistant in written, he/she would be allowed to do so.

Achievement

Until the first deployment of the project, it has achieved a good amount of success in learning many new things and updating new additional features. In the field of computing the program which are voice commanded and able to answer the question speech recognition has done a remarkable job. Hopping to continue with the project to enhance it to a better and sophisticated way of processing.
